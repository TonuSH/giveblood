Who We Are?

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower communities by providing a seamless and accessible blood donation experience. Through dedicated efforts, we strive to ensure a constant supply of life-saving blood, meeting the critical needs of patients in our region.

Our Vision

We envision a future where blood donation is a celebrated act of community service, readily accessible to all eligible individuals. By leveraging technology and fostering community partnerships, we aim to eliminate blood shortages and ensure timely access to blood products for every patient in need.

How to Donate

1

Check Eligibility

Ensure that you meet the eligibility criteria for blood donation. Common requirements include age, weight, and overall health.

2

Make an Appointment

Schedule an appointment at your nearest blood donation center or upcoming blood drive. Walk-in donations may also be available.

3

Prepare for Donation

Stay hydrated, eat a healthy meal, and get a good night's sleep before your donation appointment. Bring a valid ID and any required forms.

4

Donate Blood

Arrive at the donation center on time and follow the instructions of the staff. The donation process typically takes less than an hour.

5

Rest and Refresh

After donating, take a few minutes to rest and enjoy a complimentary refreshment. Avoid strenuous activities for the rest of the day.
